      About this wiki

      Data sources

      Species data is sourced from the IUCN Red List, Wikidata, and Wikipedia, supplemented by peer-reviewed literature. Population trends, threat assessments, and vital statistics reflect the most recent published assessments at time of entry.

      Data tiers
      Full Complete profile: all data fields, photos, threats, health metrics, vital signs, and action items.
      Standard All data fields present, but no photos yet.
      Stub Basic info only: name, status, description, and habitat. More data to come.
      Health score

      The health % is a heuristic derived from a species' IUCN conservation status, calibrated to reflect population viability: Least Concern ≈ 82%, Near Threatened ≈ 62%, Vulnerable ≈ 45%, Endangered ≈ 27%, Critically Endangered ≈ 10%. Individual scores are fine-tuned using population trend data where available.

      Contribute or report errors

      Found outdated data, a missing species, or a factual error? Please open an issue on GitHub or reach out directly. Contributions to expand stub entries to full profiles are especially welcome.
